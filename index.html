Cave Building Construction

Development of both residential and commuinty based artifical caves offered with planning , design, building regulations and contract adminstration. We are the minds behind the development and deployment of some of the most famous caves in the country with unique designs intended to be occupied and used for generations to come. Artificial cave engineering in Africa is a relatively new concept and not many examples exist on the continent. The process of creating an artificial cave involves a combination of excavation, rock-cutting, and construction techniques. The engineer must consider factors such as the stability of the ground and the structural integrity of the artificial cave. Additionally, proper ventilation, lighting and drainage systems must be designed to ensure the safety and comfort of visitors. We take pride in being able to develop this type of cave from planning to regulation giving a safe and warm inviting ambiance for generations to come for Nigerians and other visitors.
