Our Founder

Issi Jude

Issi Jude is a Nigerian based Artist born in Delta State Nigeria. He acheived his higher National diploma in Painting in the school of art and design Auchi Polythenic between 1996-2000. He has been applying his knowledge in art as a proffession since graduation. He has organised and attended several art exhibition. As a colorist who has done a lot more experimentation with colours which revives a great deal of bold approach in his works. He is also a naturalist in his plarticular ways of monochromatic painting which gives a heart dazzling effect. Due to the creative ability in him he has also ventured into the area of metal works with a found success. Issi Jude has proven that the human brain hasno limit in dequiring intelect by using his talent and creative ablility to venture into fabtrication and construction of sculptures in cubism.
