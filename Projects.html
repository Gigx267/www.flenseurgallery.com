Famous Works & Achievements

As an artist with so many achievements throughout his day in artisy

Issi Jude has successfully branched into various forms of art which have created a national name for him

Past Famous Works

Logo for National Centre of Women Development

In a competition Issi Jude won the award for designing the logo for NCWD - (National Centre of Women Development ) for the women comissioned by the
Former 1st Lady Maryam Abacha in the 90s as a student of art before his graduation. Starting off his climb to great heights from just a very early stage of his life.

Scuplture of the National Youth Service Corps

He rebuilt the outstanding scuplture of the National Youth Service Corps which can be seen in there Headquaters , followed by the metallic typography which is art of arranging type to make written language legible, readable and appealing when displayed. This signature can be seen displayed at the Yakubu Gowon NYSC HQ , where he added a sense of luxury, elegance, and modernity to it thereby bringing it alive to the people.

Mural Sculpture of the lady justice

He designed and built in cubism the Muti Mural Sculpture of the lady justice which can be seen in the jabi high court located at fct abuja is responsible for dispensing justice to the citizens of the FCT, which is home to over 2.5 million people.

Kapadoccia Cave Restarurant

He was invited and commissioned to build the very first man made cave in Africa
(cave restarurant) call Kapadoccia which can been found in wuse 2 Fct Abuja. This work was also followed by the construction of the second cave restarurant in lagos , Kapadoccia cave restarurant lagos within Taylor street victoria island. These series of restarurants is one of the very top places for fine dining in the country.

Water fountain at Nigeria Police Force HQ

He has taken art and creativity as a way of life. He created a water fountain called "fountain fall" by combining natural roles together in a systematic arrangment to put together an expression of the fall of various water fountains. This work can be seen at the louis edet house at the nigerian police force Headquaters
